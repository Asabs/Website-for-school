Our Features

Every school has some good features that we look for and Madolly School has it own oustanding features.

Subjects

Mathematics, English Language, Social Studies, Integrated Science and other subjects are crucial in Madolly School. Also, subjects such as P/E for keeping the body fit is also important in the School.

Expert Teachers

Madolly School has skilled and well trained teachers who show great love and care to student and not only that but also build students to become outstanding members of the society

E-Learning

Madolly School uses the e-learning method to sometimes teach the students. It is believed that the human brain can easily remember and relate to what is seen and heard via moving pictures or videos.

Lessons

Lessons in the school are held at specific and comfortable time including breaks for student to relax and enjoy their snacks.

Events & Sports

Aside academics, Madolly takes high interest in events and sports. Events such as cultural dances and anniversaries are key to the school and not only that but also athletics and football.

Professional Certificate

Madolly School gives professional certificates to students who advance from elementary, preschool, basic school and even JHS. The school also awards certificate to students who are too be honoured
